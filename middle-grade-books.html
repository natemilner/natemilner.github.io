DINOSORCERERS

A group of dinosaur wizards known as Dinosorcerers escaped extinction 65 million years ago by fleeing the Earth. Now they’re back and ready to reclaim the planet they once ruled.

School is almost in session at Dinosorcerers Academy, where dinosaur wizards can practice their craft, but there’s one problem…the dinosorcerers are nowhere to be found.

Is it the work of Occidor, the evil Corythosaurus? Did the absent-minded Iguanodon Beeon simply misplace them? And what does it all have to do with the academy's sudden mysterious infestation of birds? Our intrepid heroes, Charlie and Meredith, will answer these questions and more in…
